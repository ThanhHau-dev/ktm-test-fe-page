DUKE 390

OVERVIEW

KTM Duke 390 is a sports-naked bike with a starting price of Rs 3.11 Lakh. It is available in India in 1 variant and 2 colours with high end variant price starting from Rs 3.11 Lakh. Duke 390 is powered by a 398.63 cc BS6-2.0 engine which develops a power of 46 PS and a torque of 39 Nm. It has Disc front brakes and Disc rear brakes. The weight of KTM Duke 390 is 168.3 kg and comes with a fuel tank capacity of 15 L.

Designed by Thanh Hau
